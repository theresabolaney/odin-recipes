Best Salmon Bake

Description

It's quick, easy, and healthy! Sourced from MAGGIE1205 at allrecipes.com

Original recipe page found here.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon fillet, halved
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 5 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place salmon on a lightly oiled sheet pan or in a shallow baking dish, folding under thin outer edges of fillets for even cooking.
  3. Top salmon with chopped tomatoes and green onions, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook salmon in the preheated oven, uncovered, until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes.
